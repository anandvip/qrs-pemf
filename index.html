Objection: "This seems expensive. Why choose PEMF over other treatments?"

Objection: "I'm worried about side effects or interactions with my medications."
Response: PEMF is generally very safe with minimal side effects. It doesn't introduce chemicals into your body, just stimulates natural healing processes. Always consult your doctor, especially if you have implanted electrical devices like pacemakers. We can provide detailed safety information for you to discuss with your healthcare provider.
